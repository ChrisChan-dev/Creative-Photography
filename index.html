Story Behind the Image

Nature provides a canvas that is constantly in motion...

Where every frame holds a unique intersection of light and life...

This collection explores the quiet beauty of our natural world.

Photography Principles Used

Composition

The rule of thirds was applied by placing the main visual elements off-center to create balance.

Lighting

Natural light from the window was used to create soft shadows and a calm mood.

Focus & Depth of Field

The main subject is in sharp focus while the background remains simple to avoid distractions.

Color & Contrast

Subtle contrast between light and shadow enhances the image’s depth and visual interest.

Post-Processing

Brightness and contrast were slightly adjusted to maintain a natural and clean look.
