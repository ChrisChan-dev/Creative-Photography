Story Behind the Image

This collection serves as a visual odyssey, documenting a journey that spans the sacred pagodas of Cebu, the mist-shrouded pine forests of Baguio, and the vibrant urban oases found in Kaohsiung.

We sought to capture the profound dialogue between history and the natural world, from the rhythmic, curtain-like descent of Tumalog Falls to the preserved, century-old railway tracks of Kaohsiung Port Station that still remain usable today.

As we moved from the colorful architectural heritage of the Bell Church to the turquoise depths of Kawasan, we witnessed a delicate balance between man-made spirituality and raw, unfiltered nature.

Whether observing kitesurfing at Bulabog Beach or the stoic, perfect cone of Mayon Volcano, each frame represents a moment of stillness amidst a world in motion, inviting the viewer to appreciate the aesthetic harmony found in every corner of our landscape.

Photography Principles Used

Composition

Utilized leading lines through stone pathways and bridges to guide the eye toward architectural focal points like the pagoda and altar.

Lighting

Harnessing soft, diffused mountain light to capture the vibrant greens of the bamboo and the intricate details of temple rooftops under overcast skies.

Focus & Depth of Field

Selective focus was applied to the blooming sunflowers and lotus ponds, allowing busy city backgrounds to melt into a soft, secondary layer.

Color & Contrast

Juxtaposing the bright reds and teals of the sacred architecture against the deep, organic greens of the Baguio pine forests.

Post-Processing

Enhanced clarity to emphasize the texture of the stone steps and the ethereal mist surrounding the multi-tiered waterfalls.
